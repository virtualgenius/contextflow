The Focus button seeds a proximity view on the selected context. It toggles: press again (or use the focus bar's ×) to clear. Focus is transient working state, like zoom — it isn't saved with the project.

Notes: the canvas, top bar, and inspector reuse the app's real Tailwind classes. The dimming reuses the same treatment the app already applies to non-connected contexts when you select a group or relationship. Hop default is +1. Everything here is the first slice only.
