Featuring the Buckminster Fuller quote in Help

The quote is ContextFlow's thesis in one sentence: the tool exists to change how teams think about their systems, not to lecture them about DDD. Three placement options below, each rendered in the app's existing visual language. They are not mutually exclusive; A pairs naturally with B or C.

"If you want to teach people a new way of thinking, don't bother trying to teach them. Instead, give them a tool, the use of which will lead to new ways of thinking." Richard Buckminster Fuller

New surface to build and to suppress once any context exists; A and B reuse existing components.

Users who start from a sample project never see it.

Recommendation: B as the anchor (full quote, right moment), with A's shortened footer line as a persistent echo. C is worth keeping in mind if an empty-state ever gets built anyway.
